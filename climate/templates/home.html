Datasets

"Harness the Power of Climate Data for In-Depth Analysis and Forecasting."

CMIP6

CMIP6 provides crucial global climate projections from multiple models, supporting climate research and policy by offering data on temperature, precipitation, and more under various emission scenarios.

CORDEX

CORDEX focuses on regional climate downscaling, providing high-resolution climate projections tailored to specific regions, helping to assess local climate impacts and inform adaptation strategies.

CMIP7

CMIP7, the next phase of the project, will enhance climate modeling capabilities, providing more accurate and detailed future climate scenarios to support global climate assessments and informed policy decisions.
