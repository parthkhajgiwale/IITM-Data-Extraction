Why Climate Data Extraction Tool?
This tool allows researchers, policymakers, and developers to interactively select geographical coordinates and download or visualize climate variables (like temperature, precipitation, etc.) from major datasets such as CMIP6, CMIP7, and CORDEX for historical and future projections.
What datasets are used in this Tool?
CMIP6(Coupled Model Intercomparison Project Phase 6) provides global climate model outputs used in IPCC AR6.
CMIP7 is the upcoming phase with enhanced resolution and model realism, supporting AR7.
CORDEX (Coordinated Regional Downscaling Experiment) offers regionally downscaled versions of CMIP data for higher spatial detail.
What climate variables and timeframe are supported in the tool?
Common climate variables include temperature, precipitation, humidity, wind speed, and others. Supported timeframes typically range from historical (1950s–2020s) to future projections (2021–2100) under different scenarios (SSPs or RCPs).
Can I compare data across models or scenarios within the tool?
Yes, the tool supports multi-model comparison and scenario overlay features. You can visualize how different models or scenarios respond to climate drivers over time or across spatial domains.
Is the tool web based or do I need to install anything?
Yes, it is entirely web-based. No installation is required—just access it via a modern browser. Data processing is handled on the backend using RESTful APIs. User need to Login to get complete access of the tool.
I forgot my Password, how can I reset it?
If you’ve forgotten your password, click on the Forgot Password? link on the login page. You’ll be prompted to enter your registered email address. A secure password reset link will be sent to your email.
